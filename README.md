    <h1>Who Are We?</h1> 
     <p>JOI is an organization within our school that works to teach students of leadership within their community, through service events, and other academic opportunities, and help serve our community to make it a better place.
<a href="https://www.cerritosoptimistclub.com/">Cerritos Optimist Club</a> is another organization that we assimilate with through events, and we work together to provide a cared space for our community.</p>
